ABOUT ME

I am currently a second year graduate student at Michigan State University in the department of Physics and Astronomy.

I work with Professor Wolfgang Kerzendorf searching for surviving companions to type Ia supernova systems to try to uncover their explosion mechanisms. To do this I use statistical methods to analyze large datasets of stars in supernova remnants.
